About Me

Thank you for visiting my page! I am Rachel Garland the face behind Rachel's Handcrafted Soap.

I am originally from metro Detroit but currently reside in Charlotte, Michigan with my husband and son, Liam.

My husband and I met in college at Central Michigan University where I graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Biology.

After a lifelong passion for bath and body products and love for crafts, I developed an interest in creating handmade soap. In the fall of 2014 I started to make handmade soap and now you can find it here along with other bath and body goodies.
